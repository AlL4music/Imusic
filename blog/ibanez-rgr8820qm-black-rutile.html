Ibanez RGR8820QM j.custom in Black Rutile: A Japanese Metal Masterpiece

Three decades of metal have been forged into one guitar. The Ibanez RGR8820QM j.custom pairs a stunning Black Rutile quilted-maple top with Fishman Fluence electronics and the legendary Lo-Pro Edge tremolo — a hand-built, made-in-Japan instrument built for speed, strength and stage-ready precision.

Some guitars are built to a price. The j.custom line is built to a standard. Manufactured by an elite group of Ibanez luthiers, every j.custom represents the best woods, the finest fret work, and the top-tier hardware the company has developed over the decades. The RGR8820QM is the RG shape at its absolute peak — and part of Ibanez's forward-looking Axe Design Lab series.

That Black Rutile finish

The headline is the look. A 4mm quilted maple top over an alder body catches the light in deep, smoky waves, framed in a natural binding and finished in gloss. It's paired with gold hardware throughout for a genuinely luxurious, understated-but-deadly aesthetic. Tonally, that quilted-maple/alder combination delivers a bright, well-balanced voice with enriched resonance and sustain.

A neck built for speed

Play it and the Super Wizard AS 5-piece Maple/Wenge neck is the first thing you'll feel. Its asymmetric profile — rounder on the bass side, slim overall (17 mm at the nut, 19 mm at the 12th) — makes sweeping and string-skipping feel effortless. Add a rosewood fretboard, 24 jumbo frets with j.custom edge treatment for that glass-smooth glide, and the breathtaking Tree of Life inlay, and you have one of the most playable necks Ibanez makes.

Fishman Fluence firepower

Under the hood, a pair of Fishman® Fluence™ Modern Humbucker (Ceramic) pickups deliver an aggressive, powerful attack with none of the noise of traditional pickups. The clever Voicing switch flips them from a modern, active high-output roar to a crisp, clean, fluid voice — two guitars in one. It's controlled simply: 1 volume, 1 tone and a 3-way lever.

Hardware that stays in tune

The Lo-Pro Edge tremolo is the stuff of legend — a streamlined, low-profile double-locking bridge with recessed fine tuners and locking studs for rock-solid tuning stability, no matter how hard you dive. Gotoh® machine heads and a locking nut keep everything locked in, Luminlay side dots glow on dark stages, and Gotoh® strap-lock pins keep it safely on your shoulder. It ships in a hardshell case with tremolo arm, multi-tool and certificate.

Who is it for?

The RGR8820QM is for the serious player — the metal and progressive guitarist who wants a lifetime instrument that looks as extraordinary as it plays. If you've owned RGs before and want the very best one, this is it.
